विश्वकर्मा योजना लाभार्थी सूची 2024 – PM Vishwakarma Beneficiary List

The Vishwakarma Yojana 2024 aims to uplift and empower a diverse range of artisans, encompassing 18 distinct fields of expertise. The beneficiary list embraces the skilled hands of carpenters, boat makers, blacksmiths, locksmiths, and goldsmiths, whose intricate craftsmanship has been passed down through generations.

Additionally, it extends its support to potters, cobblers, footwear artisans, and toy makers, whose creations serve practical purposes and carry the essence of cultural traditions.

The initiative further recognizes the invaluable contributions of raajmistris, whose architectural prowess has adorned our landscapes with timeless structures. This comprehensive program seeks to preserve and promote the rich tapestry of India’s artisanal heritage, ensuring that these time-honoured skills continue to thrive and inspire future generations.

The identification of beneficiaries for the PM Vishwakarma Yojana is based on the artisans who have registered themselves on the eShram portal, falling under any of the eligible trades recognized by the scheme.

However, it is important to note that individuals who wish to register for the PM Vishwakarma Yojana will not be able to verify their names or eligibility status online through the eShram-Portal.

The process of beneficiary selection and verification is conducted through dedicated channels established by the relevant authorities, ensuring a fair and transparent assessment of each application.

Applicants are advised to follow the official guidelines and procedures outlined by the government to determine their eligibility and facilitate a smooth registration process for the PM Vishwakarma Yojana.

The government’s official website provides the option to download a comprehensive PDF document containing the Vishwakarma Yojana beneficiary list, organized by state and district while preserving the anonymity of individual beneficiaries.

How to check the name in the PM Vishwakarma Beneficiary List?

While you cannot directly verify your name on the beneficiary list, you have the opportunity to register yourself at the eShram Portal. Alternatively, you can seek assistance in confirming your eligibility by visiting your nearest Common Service Center (CSC) or Gram Panchayat office, where dedicated personnel can guide you through the process.

The government’s open data portal, data.gov.in, offers transparency by providing access to the beneficiary list of the Vishwakarma Yojana, categorized by state, district, gender, age, and primary occupation. However, to maintain privacy, individual names are omitted from this publicly available list.

For those seeking a comprehensive understanding of the process, here are the detailed step-by-step instructions to access the complete beneficiary list:

  1. Access the eShram Portal: Visit the official eShram Portal by following this link: https://eshram.gov.in
  2. Navigate to the data.gov.in Link: Scroll down on the eShram Portal and click on the “eShram Data Access” link as shown in the image.
  3. Explore the eShram Data Page: This link will redirect you to a page that resembles the one displayed in the image. (see the image above)
  4. Download the Beneficiary List: To view the beneficiary list of the Vishwakarma Yojana, select your State, District, Gender, and the eligible trade/occupation from the Vishwakarma Yojana list. Then, click the “Preview & Download” button.
    Upon clicking this button, you will see a screen similar to the one shown in the image. Click the “Download” button to obtain the list in PDF or CSV format. However, to download the PDF, you need to log in or create an account on data.gov.in.
  5. Register on data.gov.in: To register on data.gov.in, you need to create a Single Sign-On ID at the Indian government’s Meri Pehchan portal using the following link: [Meri Pehchan Portal Link].
  6. Complete Registration on Meri Pehchan Portal: You can register by providing your basic details, such as your name, mobile number, and address. Additionally, you will need to complete the KYC (Know Your Customer) process by authenticating your Aadhaar number using the One-Time Password (OTP) received.
  7. Log in to data.gov.in and Download the List: After successfully registering on the Meri Pehchan Portal, you can log in to data.gov.in and proceed to download the desired beneficiary list.
