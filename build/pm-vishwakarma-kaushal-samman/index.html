PM Vishwakarma Kaushal Samman

According to the information provided by the Chhattisgarh Public Relations Department on December 24, 2023, eligible individuals working in 18 traditional occupations in the state will receive benefits under the ‘PM Vishwakarma Kaushal Samman Yojana’ launched by Prime Minister Narendra Modi.

Absolutely! Here are comprehensive UPSC CSE notes on the PM Vishwakarma Yojana:

PM VISHWAKARMA SHRAM SAMMAN OVERVIEW:

  • Launched on September 17, 2023 (Vishwakarma Jayanti)
  • Central Sector Scheme by the Ministry of Skill Development and Entrepreneurship
  • Time Period: Five years (FY 2023-24 to FY 2027-28)
  • Budget: Rs. 13,000 – 15,000 Crore

Aim of PM Vishwakarma Shram Samman

  • To uplift and empower traditional artisans and craftspeople (Vishwakarmas)
  • Preserve India’s rich cultural heritage
  • Integrate artisans into the formal economy and global value chains
  • Enhance their socio-economic status and quality of life

Target Beneficiaries of PM Vishwakarma Shram Samman

  • Artisans and craftspeople involved in 18 specified trades under the scheme:
    • Blacksmiths
    • Goldsmiths
    • Potters
    • Carpenters
    • Sculptors
    • Stone carvers
    • Masons
    • Basket/mat/broom makers/coir weavers
    • Cobblers
    • Doll & toy makers
    • Barbers
    • Garland makers
    • Washermen
    • Tailors
    • Fishermen
    • Boat makers
    • Armorers

Key Features and Benefits of PM Vishwakarma Shram Samman

  1. PM Vishwakarma Certificate and ID Card: Formal recognition of artisans’ skills and craftsmanship.
  2. Credit Support:
    • Collateral-free credit up to ₹1 lakh (first tranche) and ₹2 lakh (second tranche).
    • Concessional interest rate of 5%.
  3. Skill Upgradation:
    • Basic and advanced skill training.
    • Stipend of ₹500 per day during training.
    • Toolkit incentive of ₹15,000.
  4. Digital Transactions and Marketing Support:
    • Cashback incentives for digital transactions.
    • Training and support for e-commerce and marketing.
  5. Health Insurance: Coverage under Pradhan Mantri Suraksha Bima Yojana for accidental death or disability.

Implementation of PM Vishwakarma Shram Samman

  • Registration through Common Service Centers (CSCs) or online portal.
  • Skill verification and assessment by trained professionals.
  • Training through recognized institutions and workshops.
  • Disbursement of loans and incentives through designated channels.

Challenges to PM Vishwakarma Shram Samman

  • Reaching and identifying eligible artisans in remote areas.
  • Ensuring effective skill training and upgradation.
  • Establishing sustainable market linkages and demand for products.
  • Preventing misuse of funds and ensuring transparency.

Significance of PM Vishwakarma Shram Samman

  • Empowers a significant workforce in India, particularly those from marginalized communities.
  • Preserves traditional crafts and knowledge systems.
  • Promotes inclusive growth and reduces income disparities.
  • Enhances India’s global competitiveness in the handicrafts sector.

Relevance for UPSC CSE

  • GS II: Government policies and interventions for development in various sectors and issues arising out of their design and implementation.
  • GS III: Indian Economy and issues relating to planning, mobilization of resources, growth, development, and employment.

Additional Points

  • The scheme aligns with the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), particularly those related to poverty reduction, decent work, and economic growth.
  • It complements other government initiatives like Skill India Mission and Make in India.
